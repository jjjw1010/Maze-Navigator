Final Demo

In the Final Demo, "The Robot" would have to explore through the maze where it would need to find 3 treasures which are blinking IR treasures each at different frequenices. When the treasure is found, "The Robot" will measure the blinking frequency using a specific method and will send the frequency to the base station wirelessly. The base station will display the frequency of the treasures as they are discovered. Exploration is timed until the last treasure is found and its frequency is displayed.

Labs in Preparation for Final Demo

  • Lab 1: Arduino Intro, Servos & Ultrasonic Sensors
  • Lab 2: Phototransistors & Displays
  • Lab 3: Filtering & Fourier
  • Lab 4: RF Comms & Navigation
